Born and raised in a small town, Bahawalnagar. Often mistaken as Indian but I am Pakistani by nationality and citizen of world by passion. Resident in Enschede, The Netherlands since 2013. Currently, a <b>PhD Candidate</b> at <b>Department of Construction Management and Engineering at University of Twente</b>. Hold a <b>Master degree in Computer Science</b>, in the field of information system engineering from University of Twente. Also obtained a <b>gold-medal</b> for my bachelor studies at The Islamia University of Bahawalpur, Bahawalnagar Campus. An active member board member of Pakistani Student Association at UT from last four years. 

My research interests are in infrastructure management of roads and railways specifically for their maintenance planning and decision-making.  Currently, I am researching the application of multi-criteria decision analysis and machine learning methods to assist in decision-making processes for maintenance planning. I am involved in two EU projects, namely, DESTination RAIL and TU1406 COST Action. Other than that, I am interested to learn and polish my skills for data analysis and visualizations.
